Bangladesh HSC Business Studies Syllabus

Chapter Topics
1. Introduction to Business Concept of business, Classification of business activities, Business objectives, Business environment, Business ethics and social responsibility, Business risks, Business functions.
2. Forms of Business Organization Sole proprietorship, Partnership, Joint stock company, Cooperative society, Public and private sector enterprises, Business combinations.
3. Business Finance and Marketing Concept of finance, Financial needs and sources of funds, Financial planning, Financial management, Concept of marketing, Marketing mix, Marketing functions and institutions.
4. Production and Operations Management Production function, Production planning and control, Production process, Capacity planning, Quality control, Location and layout decisions, Inventory control, Production cost analysis.
5. Business Environment Micro and macro environment, Economic environment, Legal and political environment, Social and cultural environment, Technological environment, Competitive environment, Business cycle.
6. Business Ethics and Corporate Social Responsibility Concept of business ethics, Ethical issues in business, Corporate social responsibility, Code of conduct, Consumer protection, Environmental protection, Stakeholder management.
7. Business Accounting Introduction to accounting, Accounting principles and concepts, Accounting equation, Recording of transactions, Preparation of financial statements (Income statement, Balance sheet), Analysis and interpretation of financial statements.
8. Entrepreneurship and Small Business Concept of entrepreneurship, Entrepreneurial qualities and skills, Entrepreneurial process, Small business management, Role of small businesses in economic development, Government support for small businesses.
9. Business Communication Concept of communication, Communication process, Types of communication (Verbal, Non-verbal, Written), Business correspondence, Report writing, Presentation skills, Communication barriers.
10. Business Organization and Management Organizing function, Organizational structure, Departmentation, Delegation and decentralization, Management process, Principles of management, Leadership and motivation, Decision-making, Control process.
