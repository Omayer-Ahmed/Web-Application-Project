English Syllabus

Unit Topics
Unit 1: Reading Skills Reading comprehension, Vocabulary development, Note-taking strategies, Summarizing and paraphrasing, Critical reading and analysis.
Unit 2: Writing Skills Essay writing, Paragraph writing, Formal and informal letter writing, Report writing, Writing for specific purposes (e.g., application, CV), Creative writing.
Unit 3: Grammar and Vocabulary Parts of speech, Tenses, Sentence structure, Subject-verb agreement, Articles, Prepositions, Word formation, Synonyms and antonyms, Idioms and phrases.
Unit 4: Listening Skills Listening comprehension, Identifying main ideas and supporting details, Note-taking during listening, Understanding different accents and speech styles.
Unit 5: Speaking Skills Oral presentations, Group discussions, Debates, Interview skills, Pronunciation and intonation, Fluency and coherence.
Unit 6: Literature Short stories, Poems, Plays, Novel excerpts, Literary devices and techniques, Analysis and interpretation of literary texts.
Unit 7: Supplementary Reading Selected readings from various genres (e.g., articles, essays, speeches, biographies), Comprehension and analysis of supplementary texts, Developing reading skills and critical thinking.
Unit 8: Study Skills Time management, Effective note-taking, Research skills, Study techniques, Exam preparation strategies, Critical thinking and problem-solving.
