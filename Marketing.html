Bangladesh HSC Marketing Syllabus

Chapter Topics
1. Introduction to Marketing Definition and scope of marketing, Core marketing concepts, Marketing environment, Marketing mix (Product, Price, Place, Promotion), Marketing orientations (Production, Product, Sales, Marketing, Societal).
2. Marketing Planning and Strategies Marketing planning process, SWOT analysis, Marketing objectives and strategies, Target market selection, Positioning, Differentiation, Marketing segmentation, Marketing research.
3. Consumer Behavior Factors influencing consumer behavior, Consumer decision-making process, Consumer buying behavior models, Buyer characteristics and types, Market segmentation, Consumer perception and learning, Attitudes and beliefs.
4. Product Planning and Development Product life cycle, Product mix, Product line, Branding and packaging, New product development, Product differentiation and positioning, Product adoption process.
5. Pricing Strategies Pricing objectives, Factors influencing pricing decisions, Pricing strategies (Cost-based pricing, Market-based pricing, Value-based pricing), Pricing methods (Cost-plus pricing, Penetration pricing, Skimming pricing), Price elasticity of demand.
6. Distribution and Channel Management Channel functions and types, Channel intermediaries, Channel design and management, Physical distribution and logistics, Retailing and wholesaling, E-commerce and online distribution.
7. Integrated Marketing Communication Promotion mix (Advertising, Sales promotion, Personal selling, Public relations), IMC planning process, Media selection and planning, Message design and execution, Publicity and public relations.
8. Marketing Research Marketing research process, Research design, Data collection methods (Primary and secondary data), Sampling techniques, Data analysis and interpretation, Marketing information system.
9. Social Responsibility and Ethics in Marketing Marketing ethics, Socially responsible marketing, Ethical decision-making in marketing, Consumer rights and protection, Consumerism, Green marketing.
10. International Marketing Global marketing environment, Global marketing strategies, International market entry modes, Export marketing, International marketing mix, Standardization and adaptation.
