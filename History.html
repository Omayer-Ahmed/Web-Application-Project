Period Topics
Ancient Period Prehistoric age, Indus Valley Civilization, Vedic age, Maurya Empire, Gupta Empire, Harsha Empire, Ancient trade and urbanization, Buddhism and Jainism, Ancient art and architecture.
Medieval Period Arrival of Muslims in Bengal, Delhi Sultanate, Sufism in Bengal, Bengal under Mughal rule, European trade and colonization, Rise of regional powers (Bengal Nawabs, Marathas), Language and literature in medieval Bengal.
British Period Arrival of the British East India Company, British colonial rule in Bengal, Socio-economic impact of British rule, Indian National Movement in Bengal, Partition of Bengal, Swadeshi Movement, Non-cooperation Movement, Civil disobedience movement, Quit India Movement.
Partition and Independence Partition of India and creation of Pakistan, Language movement, 1952, Formation of East Pakistan, Language and cultural movements in East Pakistan, Six-point movement, 1971 Liberation War, Formation of Bangladesh.
Post-Independence Bangladesh Constitutional developments, Political parties and governance, Economic development and challenges, Social and cultural changes, Foreign relations and international diplomacy, War crimes trials, Women's rights and empowerment.
Global History World history from ancient to modern times, Major civilizations and empires, Global trade and cultural exchanges, Industrial revolution, World wars and their impact, Decolonization and post-colonial world, United Nations and global governance.
Historiography Concept of history, Historical sources and methods, Interpretation and writing of history, Role of historians, Debates and controversies in history, Importance of studying history.
