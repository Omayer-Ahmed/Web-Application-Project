Gofagaon Government Collage

GGC is a Government college that was established in 1950

Unit 1: Bangla Sahityer Itihas (History of Bengali Literature) Early and Medieval Bengali Literature, Renaissance Literature, Modern Bengali Literature, Major literary movements, Prominent poets, novelists, playwrights, and essayists.
Unit 2: Bangla Sahityer Ruprekha (Literary Criticism) Concept of literary criticism, Major theories and approaches, Forms of literary criticism (Biographical, Historical, Formalist, Structuralist, Postcolonial, Feminist, etc.), Critical analysis of selected literary works.
Unit 3: Bangla Kobita (Poetry) Forms and genres of Bengali poetry, Prominent poets and their works, Evolution of Bengali poetry, Themes and symbolism in Bengali poetry, Critical appreciation of selected poems.
Unit 4: Bangla Upanyas (Novel) Bengali novels from different periods, Popular novelists and their works, Themes and social issues in Bengali novels, Character analysis and critical interpretation of selected novels.
Unit 5: Bangla Natok O Drame (Drama) Bengali drama and theater, Evolution of Bengali drama, Major playwrights and their works, Themes and techniques in Bengali drama, Critical analysis of selected plays.
Unit 6: Bangla Galpo O Upanyas (Short Story and Novel) Bengali short stories and novels, Notable writers and their works, Themes and narrative techniques in Bengali short stories and novels, Critical analysis of selected stories and novels.
Unit 7: Bangla Chhotogalpo (Short Stories) Bengali short stories, Prominent short story writers and their works, Themes and narrative styles in Bengali short stories, Critical appreciation of selected short stories.
Unit 8: Bangla Golpo (Essays) Bengali essays, Renowned essayists and their works, Themes and styles in Bengali essays, Critical analysis of selected essays.
