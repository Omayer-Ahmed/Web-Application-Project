Bangladesh HSC Chemistry Syllabus

Chapter Topics
1. Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry Matter and its properties, Laws of chemical combinations, Atomic and molecular masses, Mole concept and molar mass, Percentage composition, Empirical and molecular formula, Stoichiometry and calculations based on stoichiometry.
2. States of Matter Gaseous state, Ideal gas equation, Kinetic theory of gases, Deviation from ideal behavior, Liquids and solids, The structure of solids, Types of solids, Amorphous and crystalline solids, Polymorphism, Allotropy.
3. Chemical Energetics and Thermodynamics Energy changes during a chemical reaction, Enthalpy, Hess's law, Enthalpy of different types of reactions, Spontaneity of a chemical reaction, Entropy and free energy, Gibbs energy change for a spontaneous and non-spontaneous process, Equilibrium constant (Kc and Kp), Le Chatelier's principle.
4. Chemical Equilibrium Equilibrium in physical and chemical processes, Law of mass action, Equilibrium constants (Kc and Kp), Factors affecting equilibrium, Le Chatelier's principle and its applications, Ionic equilibrium, Acids, bases, and salts, Ionization of acids and bases, pH scale, Buffer solutions.
5. Electrochemistry Redox reactions, Electrochemical cells, Electrolysis, Conductance of electrolytic solutions, Nernst equation, Corrosion, Batteries and fuel cells.
6. Chemical Kinetics Rate of a chemical reaction, Factors affecting the rate of a reaction, Integrated rate equations, Pseudo first-order reaction, Arrhenius equation, Activation energy, Catalysis.
7. Organic Chemistry Hydrocarbons, Classification, Nomenclature, Isomerism, Alkanes, Alkenes, Alkynes, Aromatic hydrocarbons, IUPAC nomenclature, General methods of preparation, Properties, Uses of hydrocarbons, Haloalkanes and Haloarenes.
8. Environmental Chemistry Environmental pollution, Air pollution, Water pollution, Soil pollution, Industrial waste, Green chemistry, Ozone depletion, Acid rain, Global warming, Water treatment.
