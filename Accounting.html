Bangladesh HSC Accounting Syllabus

Chapter Topics
1. Introduction to Accounting Definition and scope of accounting, Objectives of accounting, Accounting principles and concepts, Accounting equation, Accounting cycle.
2. Accounting Principles and Concepts Accounting principles, Accounting concepts, Accounting standards, Double-entry system, Rules of debit and credit.
3. Recording of Transactions Journal, Ledger, Trial balance, Subsidiary books (Cash book, Sales book, Purchase book, etc.), Rectification of errors.
4. Preparation of Financial Statements Trading account, Profit and loss account, Balance sheet, Adjustments in final accounts, Depreciation accounting.
5. Bank Reconciliation Statement Meaning and need for bank reconciliation, Preparation of bank reconciliation statement, Treatment of unpresented and outstanding checks, Errors in bank reconciliation.
6. Bills of Exchange Meaning and features of bills of exchange, Promissory notes, Endorsement, Dishonor and noting of bills, Renewal and discounting of bills.
7. Financial Statements Analysis Meaning and objectives of financial statement analysis, Tools and techniques of financial statement analysis (Ratio analysis, Comparative statements, etc.), Interpretation of financial statements.
8. Partnership Accounts Partnership deed, Admission of a partner, Retirement and death of a partner, Profit and loss appropriation account, Revaluation of assets and liabilities, Goodwill and its valuation.
9. Joint Stock Company Accounts Meaning and features of a joint stock company, Issue and forfeiture of shares, Issue and redemption of debentures, Final accounts of a company, Preparation of cash flow statement.
10. Accounting for Non-profit Organizations Meaning and features of non-profit organizations, Receipts and payments account, Income and expenditure account, Balance sheet.
