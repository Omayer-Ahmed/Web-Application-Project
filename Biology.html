Bangladesh HSC Biology Syllabus

Chapter Topics
1. Diversity of Living Organisms Introduction to diversity, Classification and hierarchy, Plant kingdom, Animal kingdom, Structural organization of plants and animals.
2. Structure and Function of Cells Cell theory, Prokaryotic and eukaryotic cells, Ultrastructure of cells, Cell organelles and their functions, Cell division.
3. Plant Physiology Transport in plants, Mineral nutrition, Photosynthesis, Respiration, Plant growth and development, Plant hormones.
4. Human Physiology Digestive system, Respiratory system, Circulatory system, Excretory system, Nervous system, Endocrine system, Reproductive system, Human development and growth.
5. Genetics Mendelian inheritance, Chromosomal theory of inheritance, DNA and RNA structure, Protein synthesis, Genetic disorders.
6. Evolution Origin of life, Evidences of evolution, Theories of evolution, Human evolution, Speciation.
7. Ecology Organisms and their environment, Ecosystems, Biodiversity and conservation, Environmental issues.
8. Applied Biology Agriculture and crop improvement, Animal husbandry, Biotechnology, Human health and diseases, Microbes in human welfare.
