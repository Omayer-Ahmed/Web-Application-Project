Bangladesh HSC Finance Syllabus

Chapter Topics
1. Introduction to Finance Definition and scope of finance, Functions of finance, Financial management, Financial institutions, Money and capital markets.
2. Financial Statement Analysis Meaning and purpose of financial statement analysis, Tools and techniques of financial statement analysis (Ratio analysis, Comparative statements, etc.), Interpretation of financial statements.
3. Time Value of Money Concept of time value of money, Future value, Present value, Annuities, Perpetuities, Compounding, and discounting.
4. Risk and Return Meaning and types of risk, Measurement of risk, Risk and return relationship, Portfolio theory, Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM).
5. Capital Budgeting Meaning and importance of capital budgeting, Methods of capital budgeting (Payback period, Net Present Value, Internal Rate of Return, Profitability Index), Capital rationing, Risk analysis in capital budgeting.
6. Cost of Capital Meaning and components of cost of capital, Measurement of cost of capital (Weighted Average Cost of Capital), Marginal cost of capital, Factors affecting cost of capital.
7. Working Capital Management Meaning and components of working capital, Cash management, Receivables management, Inventory management, Financing of working capital, Determinants of working capital requirements.
8. Capital Structure Meaning and importance of capital structure, Capital structure theories (Net Income Approach, Net Operating Income Approach, Modigliani-Miller Theorem), Factors influencing capital structure decisions.
9. Dividend Policy Meaning and determinants of dividend policy, Dividend theories (Walter's Model, Gordon's Model), Dividend policies (Stable, Constant payout ratio, Residual), Dividend reinvestment plans, Stock dividends, Stock splits.
10. Financial Markets and Institutions Types of financial markets (Money market, Capital market, Primary market, Secondary market), Financial intermediaries, Regulatory authorities, Financial instruments.
