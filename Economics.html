Gofagaon Government Collage

GGC is a Government college that was established in 1950

1. Introduction to Economics Definition and scope of economics, Basic economic concepts (Scarcity, Choice, Opportunity cost), Economic systems (Capitalist, Socialist, Mixed), Circular flow of income, Production possibilities curve.
2. Microeconomics Market and types of market, Demand and supply analysis, Elasticity of demand and supply, Consumer behavior and utility analysis, Production and cost theory, Perfect competition, Monopoly, Monopolistic competition, Oligopoly.
3. Macroeconomics Aggregate demand and aggregate supply, National income accounting, Circular flow of income and expenditure, Inflation and deflation, Unemployment, Money and banking, Fiscal policy, Monetary policy, Foreign exchange market.
4. National Income and Economic Development Concept of national income, Methods of calculating national income (Income, Expenditure, and Value-added approaches), Economic indicators, Economic development and growth, Role of agriculture, industry, and service sectors in economic development.
5. International Trade and Finance Theory of comparative advantage, Balance of trade and balance of payments, Foreign exchange rate determination, Trade barriers (Tariffs, Quotas, Embargoes), Free trade versus protectionism, Trade organizations (WTO, SAFTA).
6. Government Budget and Public Finance Role of government in the economy, Types of government revenue and expenditure, Budgetary process, Public debt, Deficit financing, Public goods and externalities, Income redistribution and poverty alleviation.
7. Economic Problems of Bangladesh Population problem, Poverty and income inequality, Unemployment problem, Inflation and price stability, Agricultural and industrial problems, Energy and environmental issues, Industrialization and sustainable development.
8. Economic Policies of Bangladesh Five-year plans, Industrial policy, Trade policy, Monetary and fiscal policies, Agricultural policy, Export promotion, Investment policy, Privatization and nationalization, Poverty reduction and social safety net programs.
9. Contemporary Economic Issues Globalization and its impact, Economic integration, Technological advancements and automation, Income distribution and inequality, Financial market instability, Economic reforms and liberalization, Role of entrepreneurship.
